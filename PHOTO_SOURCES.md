# Adirondack Photo Sources & Recommendations

## Required Photos for New York Sash Adirondack Site

### Hero Images (High Priority)
1. **Main ADK Hero** (`/images/adk-landscape-hero.jpg`)
   - **Recommended:** Wide Adirondack vista with mountains and forests
   - **Sources:** 
     - Visit Adirondacks (visitadirondacks.com) - Media Kit
     - Unsplash: search "adirondack mountains", "adirondack lakes"  
     - NY State Parks Photo Library
     - Adirondack Park Agency photo collection

2. **Boonville Landscape** (`/images/boonville-landscape.jpg`)
   - **Recommended:** North Country farmland or small town with mountains
   - **Sources:** Boonville Chamber of Commerce, Oneida County Tourism

3. **Old Forge Lake** (`/images/old-forge-lake.jpg`)
   - **Recommended:** First Lake or Fulton Chain of Lakes view
   - **Sources:** Old Forge Tourism, Central Adirondack Association

4. **Inlet Lakefront** (`/images/inlet-lakefront.jpg`)
   - **Recommended:** Fourth Lake shoreline with luxury homes
   - **Sources:** Inlet Chamber, luxury real estate listings

5. **Eagle Bay Wilderness** (`/images/eagle-bay-wilderness.jpg`)
   - **Recommended:** Remote wilderness setting, pristine forest
   - **Sources:** Adirondack wilderness photography, nature photographers

## Recommended Photo Specs
- **Hero Images:** 1920x1080px minimum
- **Thumbnails:** 800x600px minimum
- **Expert Photos:** 400x400px square
- **Format:** JPG optimized for web
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for performance
